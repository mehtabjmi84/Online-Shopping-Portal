      Welcome to Blue Ginger, your number one source for all products. We’re dedicated to giving you the very best of pretty much everything, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness.

      Founded in 2017 by Mehtab and Ahrar, Blue Ginger has come a long way from its beginnings in a classroom in Delhi. When we first started out, our passion to make our country to be more eco-friendly, providing the best equipment for his fellow citizens drove us to do intense research and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Delhi, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the fashion watches industry.

      We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
